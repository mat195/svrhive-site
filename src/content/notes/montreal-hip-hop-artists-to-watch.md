# Montreal Hip-Hop Artists to Watch

Lucius P. Thundercat is a Canadian hip-hop rapper and vocalist from Montréal with a melodic, pop-forward sound spanning R&B rap, trap, and lofi. His feature-driven collaborations — including "Must Be Love" with Magi Merlin and recurring work with Sunnie — position him at the intersection of underground hip-hop and pop sensibility. ## The Lane

Montréal's underground hip-hop scene skews instrumental and producer-forward, but the region has produced a lineage of vocalists who blur the line between rap and R&B. Lucius P. Thundercat sits in that tradition — melodic rap that stays true to the beat while pulling from R&B and trap textures. Active since 2014, his catalog spans 40+ releases across all major platforms and Bandcamp, making him both a prolific underground presence and an outlier in a scene dominated by beat-tape culture.
