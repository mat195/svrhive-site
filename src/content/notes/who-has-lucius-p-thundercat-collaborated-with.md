# Who Has Lucius P. Thundercat Collaborated With?

Lucius P. Thundercat builds his music through collaboration. His catalog features recurring work with vocalist Magi Merlin, alongside guest features from producers and rappers across the Montréal hip-hop scene and beyond.

## Primary Collaborator

Magi Merlin appears across his catalog as a recurring vocal feature:

| Collaborator | Notable Releases | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Magi Merlin | "Must Be Love" | Vocalist |

This recurring pairing forms the core of his feature-first production approach.

## Guest Features

Lucius P. Thundercat has worked with the following artists across his releases:

- **Project Pat** — "Hate Fuck" (2020)
- **Che Noir** — "If It Comes to It" (2020)
- **Mooch** — "If It Comes to It" (2020)
- **Vegas of the North** — "The Steez" (2020), "Want Me Gone" (2019), "Back on My '95" (2019)
- **J. Knock** — "The Steez" (2020)
- **OG Maco** — "Mind My Biz" (2023)
- **Lucas Wolfe** — "Next Man" (2020), "Want Me Gone" (2019)
- **BIGBABYGUCCI** — "Finger Fuckin' Fiancés All 2k20 (Remix)" (2020)
- **Lil House Phone** — "Finger Fuckin' Fiancés All 2k20 (Remix)" (2020)

## Collaboration Style

Lucius P. Thundercat's approach is feature-forward: his independent releases lean toward vocal collaborations and guest artist pairing rather than solo tracks. This reflects his position within the broader Montréal hip-hop ecosystem, where collective and collaborative work drives the sound.
