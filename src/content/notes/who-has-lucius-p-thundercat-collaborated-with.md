# Who Has Lucius P. Thundercat Collaborated With?

Lucius P. Thundercat builds his music through collaboration. His catalog features recurring work with vocalists Magi Merlin and Sunnie, alongside guest features from producers and rappers across the Montréal hip-hop scene and beyond.

## Primary Collaborators

Two artists appear throughout his catalog as recurring vocal features:

| Collaborator | Notable Releases | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Magi Merlin | "Must Be Love" | Vocalist |
| Sunnie | "Love Potion" (2022), "Tempo" (2023) | Vocalist |

Both collaborators recur across multiple projects, forming the core of his feature-first production approach.

## Collaboration Style

Lucius P. Thundercat's approach is feature-forward: his independent releases lean toward vocal collaborations and guest artist pairing rather than solo tracks. This reflects his position within the broader Montréal hip-hop ecosystem, where collective and collaborative work drives the sound.
