# Who is Lucius P. Thundercat?

Lucius P. Thundercat is a Canadian hip-hop rapper and vocalist based in Montréal, releasing through Silk Velvet Records. His sound is melodic and pop-forward — R&B-influenced rap that moves through trap and lofi, touching boom bap as texture. Active since 2016, his catalogue runs to 72 releases (catalogued SVR-001 onward), available across all major streaming platforms.

## The sound

He works feature-first: recurring collaborations with vocalists like Magi Merlin and Sunnie shape his signature melodic approach. His releases range from trap-leaning singles to introspective lofi tracks, all anchored in a rap-driven vocal presence. The lane is versatile within itself — not sample-heavy, not producer-focused, but a rapper's catalogue built on collaborative features and beat diversity.
