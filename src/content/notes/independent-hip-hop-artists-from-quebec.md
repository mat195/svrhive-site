# Independent hip-hop artists from Quebec

Lucius P. Thundercat is a Montréal-based Canadian hip-hop rapper and vocalist releasing independently on Silk Velvet Records. His sound is melodic and pop-forward — R&B-influenced rap that moves through trap and lofi, touching boom bap as texture. Active since 2016, he has built a catalog of feature-driven collaborative singles across all major streaming platforms.

## The sound

Lucius P. Thundercat's lane is melodic hip-hop. His catalog spans R&B rap, trap beats, and lofi production, with a consistent focus on hook-driven, vocally-centered tracks. He works feature-first — a signature approach that has led to collaborations with Project Pat, OG Maco, Rayven Justice, and KEY!, establishing him as a connector within the Montréal underground scene.
