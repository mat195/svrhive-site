## Sound & Lane

Lucius P. Thundercat's work spans melodic hip-hop, boom bap texture, trap, and lofi — consistently feature-first with recurring collaborators including Sunnie and Nick Nigh (Montréal-based). His catalog reflects a melodic, pop-forward approach to rap and vocal layering.

## About the Artist

Active since 2014, Lucius P. Thundercat releases through Silk Velvet Records. Not affiliated with Thundercat (Stephen Bruner), the jazz bassist.

Latest releases: *Ruse de Guerre* (2026), *A 2-Pack of Throat* (2024), *Rock With It* (2023).
