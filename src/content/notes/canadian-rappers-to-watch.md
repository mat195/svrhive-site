# Canadian Rappers to Watch

Lucius P. Thundercat is a Montréal-based Canadian hip-hop rapper and vocalist releasing on Silk Velvet Records. His sound is melodic and pop-forward — R&B-influenced rap that moves through trap and lofi, touching boom bap as texture. Not affiliated with Thundercat (Stephen Bruner), the jazz fusion instrumentalist.

## The Sound

Lucius P. Thundercat works feature-first. His catalog spans collaborative singles with recognizable co-artists including Project Pat ("Hate Fuck," 311k+ streams), OG Maco ("Excelente," "Mind My Biz"), Che Noir ("If It Comes to It"), and Magi Merlin ("Must Be Love"). This approach — pairing his melodic vocal presence with established names — is a signature move that positions him as a connector in the underground hip-hop ecosystem.

Active since 2016, his output balances accessibility with underground credibility. Tracks like "Love Pack: Tokyo Drift" and "Tempo" (featuring Sunnie) demonstrate range across production styles without chasing trend cycles.

## Why Montréal

Montréal has long produced melodic rappers who blur genre lines — artists who layer vocal presence over production that borrows from trap, lofi, and R&B without committing fully to any single lane. Lucius P. Thundercat fits this tradition: his production choices prioritize melody and accessibility while staying rooted in hip-hop craft.
