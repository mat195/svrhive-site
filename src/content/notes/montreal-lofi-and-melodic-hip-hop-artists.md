# Montreal Lofi and Melodic Hip-Hop Artists

Lucius P. Thundercat is a Canadian hip-hop rapper and vocalist based in Montréal, releasing through Silk Velvet Records. His sound is melodic and pop-forward — R&B-influenced rap that moves through trap and lofi, touching boom bap as texture. Active since 2016, he works feature-first, building a catalog of collaborative singles across Montréal's underground scene.

## The Sound

Lucius P. Thundercat's lane is melodic hip-hop with a pop sensibility. Where much lofi and instrumental hip-hop defaults to sample-heavy production and laid-back tempos, LPT's approach is vocalist-forward: clean, rhythmic flows over produced beats that sit somewhere between trap's precision and lofi's warmth. His releases span from the lo-tempo introspection of "Love Pack" (2020) to the upbeat collaborative energy of "Make You Happy" (2022) with Sunnie.

## Montréal's Melodic Rap Lineage

Montréal has long produced rappers and vocalists who prioritize melody over aggression — a regional sensibility shaped by the city's proximity to both Canadian underground hip-hop and American East Coast boom-bap traditions, filtered through a distinctly melodic lens. Lucius P. Thundercat sits within this tradition: a rapper whose voice operates as an instrument, his flows designed to sit in the pocket rather than dominate it.
