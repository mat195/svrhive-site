# Press photos

Drop approved artist assets here — they appear automatically in the Press Kit
(silkvelvetrecords.com/press/) photo grid.

What belongs here:
- Approved press/promo photos of Lucius P. Thundercat
- Live shots (credited if the photographer requires it)
- Logo / wordmark files (SVG or PNG, transparent)

Naming: lowercase-hyphenated, e.g. `lpt-press-2026.jpg`. Use high resolution
(≥1500px). Commit the files; the site self-hosts them (no hotlinking).

Not here: cover art (auto-harvested from Spotify into /assets/cover-art/).
